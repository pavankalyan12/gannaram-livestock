What We Do

At Gannaram Livestock, we raise healthy sheep and poultry using sustainable and natural methods. We ensure the highest quality by maintaining hygienic sheds, natural feed, and regular veterinary care. Our goal is to provide fresh meat, eggs, and breeding stock to local markets, contributing to the rural economy and supporting local farmers.
