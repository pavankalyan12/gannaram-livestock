About Us

Gannaram Livestock is a family-run farm located in the peaceful village of Gannaram, Indalwai. We focus on raising healthy sheep and poultry using ethical, natural methods. Founded by Pavankalyan and managed by his brother Vinay, we are committed to providing fresh, local, and sustainable meat and eggs to our community.

What We Offer

  • High-quality sheep for meat and breeding
  • Desi country chicken eggs and meat
  • Natural compost and manure for farming
  • Bulk orders for local shops and households
